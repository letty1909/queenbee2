Homemade food lovingly prepared, why not come and see what the buzz is all about for yourself!

Café is open 9am-4pm Monday to Saturday and 10am-4pm on Sunday

Our café is the perfect place to enjoy a catch up with friends over tea, coffee and homemade treats. We offer a varied menu from light lunches including sandwiches, panini's to main meals.Why not try out our daily specials bringing you seasonal choice!

Breakfast

A delicious, nutricious breakfast is the best way to start the day. Whether you're after eggs all kinds of ways, we love our popular Eggs Benedict. Simple porridge with a variety of toppings, or stack of golden waffles and pancakes at the weekend, we've got you covered with our brilliant breakfasts. Why not drizzle some of our award winning honey on the breakfast of your liking!

Hot Meals

Treat yourself to a delicious hot meal from our range of authentic Scottish dishes and worldwide favourites. Seasonal delights and winter warming soup combos. Feast until your hearts content. Our menu also features dishes specially created for vegetarian and gluten free diets as well as a special menu for the wee ones. Why not try Cullen Skink this is a traditional local favourite!

Sandwiches & Cakes

Enjoy a fresh made sandwich, all sandwiches can be made with Brown or White bread or Gluten Free. We offer Baguette's and panini's with fillings of your choice. Or why not indulge in a traybake or honey and fresh cream delicacy. A selection of genuine home baking with cakes that are soo yummy you'll want to come back for more.

Hot & Cold Drinks

Because we think our coffee is just soo good we serve only large. All milk is sourced from our local dairy. We have a wide selection of drinks from Cappuccino, Breakfast Tea, Herbal Teas, or soft drinks of Coke, Lemonade, Irn Bru and Schweppes. Why not try our favourite real fruit oaty smoothies with our award winning Queen Bee Honey.

We are situated in the pictureque town of Melrose High Street, with lot of local parking available.

Due to Covid, we are adhering to social distancing rules within the cafe our tables are now spaced out two meters apart as per government guidance. A handful of our tables are situated outside with patio heaters.

Customers just love our Honey and Queen Bee Cafe!
