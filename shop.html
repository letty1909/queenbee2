The Benefits of Honey

Our Queen Bee Honey is full of nutrients and high in antioxidants. These include organic acids and phenolic compounds like flavonoids. Scientists believe combinations of these compounds gives honey it antioxidant power. They have been linked to reduced risk or heart attacks, and lower Cholesterol which is a major factor in increased risk of heart disease. Honey is great choice in reducing the symptoms of coughs with no side effects. These are just some of the benefits of honey and a great alternative to sugar.
