Welcome

At Queen Bee Café and Farm Shop, we proudly sell and serve homemade food lovingly prepared and locally sourced goodness from Scottish businesses in and around the borders area. We are also producers of fine Scottish honey, delicious in flavour and natural in properties.

Cafe & Deals

Our homemade food lovingly prepared with locally sourced fine produce. We guarantee that every meal served at Queen Bee Café is homemade and showcases the best local Scottish produce. Every cake is made from scratch using Scottish flour, local free-range eggs, and fruit from our farm when in season.
