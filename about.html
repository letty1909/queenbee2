About Us

Passionate about pollinators...

The Queen Bee Café & Farm Shop was first founded by Samuel and Isabella Reina in 2015. The name Reina also translates as Queen in Spanish and for them makes the business feel all more personal as they are both originally from Spain and met while studying.

Feeling strongly about the environment and declining bee population, they decided to do something about it. Thier passion and determination prompted them to find out more about beekeeping. They hired a team of expert beekeepers to set up their apiary in Melrose's surrounding area in the Scottish Borders. The honey produced has a unique flavour tinged by the wildflowers and heather-covered hills and glens that evokes a sense of Scotland's unspoilt wilderness.

Our Mission

We are commited to increase the bee population by 15% over the next five years.

Our beekeepers

We are very committed to our social responsibility and work with our own beekeepers and local bee farmers within the farming industry in Scotland. This gives us a good insight in what is happening within the environment and how it is impacting pollinators. We are keen to support and develop new talent with in the industry. Interested in beekeeping see our news page.

Our Hives

Here at Queen Bee we utilise the best hives for the welfare of our bees. Our hives are specially designed to nurture the bee population and specifically for keeping them safe during the cold Scottish winter months. The hives are well insulated which encourage minimal intervention from the beekeepers, reducing both stress and disease to the bees.

Did you know!...

Worker bees make 10 million foraging trips to produce just one jar of honey.
